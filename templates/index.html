Winning Proposal Tips
Start with their problem — not your skills. Show you understand their need in the first sentence.
Use real numbers. "Reduced load time by 60%" beats "improved performance" every time.
Reference their post. One specific detail proves you actually read it — most freelancers don't.
End with a question. A smart question forces a reply and shows deeper expertise than any credential.
Address their fear. Clients fear wasted money and bad hires — tackle it directly in your proposal.
